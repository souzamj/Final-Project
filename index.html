GET LOST IN THE ELEMENTS

From August 9-11, join us in Long Pond and experience the Northeast's premier camping festival! Come discover four unique stages filled with the best electronic music from every end of the spectrum. Whether it's Water, Earth, Fire, or Air, there's an element for everyone!

Every year at Elements, we strive to bring the best talents in the dance music scene all in one place - whether you love house, techno, drum & bass, live jam bands, or headbanging dubstep, you will feel right at home at Elements!

When you need a break from dancing, check out one of our many large-scale art installations, grab some food from one of our top notch food vendors, or enjoy the company of your fellow festivalgoers in one of our scheduled group activities - whether that's yoga, meditation, or just meeting new people, there are so many new friends and memories to be made!
